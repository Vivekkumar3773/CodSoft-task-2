10 years of serving communities

Swiggy is an Indian online food ordering and delivery company, founded in 2014 by Sriharsha Majety, Nandan Reddy, and Rahul Jaimini. Headquartered in Bangalore, Swiggy operates in over 580 cities across India. Initially starting as a food delivery service, Swiggy has expanded its offerings to include quick commerce services under the name Swiggy Instamart, and same-day package deliveries with Swiggy Genie. The company aims to enhance the quality of life for urban consumers by providing convenience and a wide range of services.
